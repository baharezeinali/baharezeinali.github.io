ShiftDrift

Better sleep for shift workers — on iPhone and Apple Watch.

ShiftDrift helps shift workers build healthier sleep habits no matter when their shift ends. Whether you work nights, early mornings, or rotating schedules, ShiftDrift guides you toward faster, deeper sleep with routines designed around your unique work pattern.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ShiftDrift is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect
ShiftDrift does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

Health Data
If you grant permission, ShiftDrift may access Apple Health or Apple Watch sleep data to provide personalized recommendations. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services
ShiftDrift does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy
ShiftDrift is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
